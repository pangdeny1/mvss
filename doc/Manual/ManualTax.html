Tax Calculations(Philippines)

How is the withholding tax  computed every payday? For employees whose compensations  are primarily based on their basic pay, tax is  computed  based on  below  formula.  Use this to explain  to  your employees about their withholding tax.

Example(First Payday Simi-Monthly Payroll)
Basic Pay 5,000
plus Taxable Other Income 0
plus OverTime 0
less SSS Premium 0
less HDMF Premium 0
less PhilHealth Premium 0
=  This Payroll Taxable Income 5,000
multiplied by remaining number of payday(including current payroll) 24
= estimated taxable income to earn during the year 120,000
plus Previous Taxable Income during the year 0
= Estimated Taxable Income During the Year 120,000
Less : Personal and Additional Exemption Tax Status ME1 32000+8000 = 40,000
= Estimated Net Taxable Income 80,000
refer to BIR form 1700 for Est. Tax  during the Year 10,500
less Previous Tax w/held during the year 0
=Estimated Tax to Pay 10,500
divided by remaining number of payday(including current payroll) 24
= Tax withheld during this payroll 437.5

Using this type of tax computation will allow any type of payroll period. Year-End adjustment is almost unnecessary because payroll automatically adjust its computation. Since, payroll uses anualized method of computation it is possible that employees will no longer be deducted of tax(example if new employee join the company at last quarter).
